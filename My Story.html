About Me

Life in Kigoma, Tanzania

Kigoma is a city in Tanzania, East Africa, located on the northeastern shores of Lake Tanganyika and near the borders with Burundi and the Democratic Republic of the Congo. I was born and raised in Kigoma. I didn't get to discover the beautiful things the city had to offer because I was mainly in the refugee camp.

However, I have vivid memories of engaging with a Kigoma-based ethnic tribe known as the Waha. I also recall eating Mikebuka, one of the tastiest fish in the world that can only be found in Lake Tanganyika.

From Kigoma to Wisconsin

My family and I got the opportunity to pursue a better life in the United States in 2016. When I discovered that Wisconsin had been assigned to us as a state. I spent almost an hour comparing Tanzania and Wisconsin maps. In other words, I assumed we'd been assigned to Tanzania again.

I came to Wisconsin during the winter and had numerous challenges, including learning English and adapting to the American educational system.

<

South Division High School to the University of Wisconsin-Madison

I attended South Division High School and had the opportunity to meet with students who were in the same situation as me. In other words, the vast majority of us were all learning how to adapt.

I am presently a sophomore at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, studying Information Science and pursuing a Data Science Certificate.
